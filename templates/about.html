About Indus-Mega Farms

Founded in 2019, Indus-Mega Farms is a gap-bridger between industrial markets and small farms. We encourage both small and large holder farmers to produce better by creating a working zero-waste/funding farming system in Nigeria. We also have built platforms to make agriculture attractive to young minds in Nigerian universities.

About Indus-Mega Farms


Vision: “Making Agriculture better and attractive in Africa.”

Missions:
  1. We help farmers sell their products on time by connecting them to a larger market.
  2. We also produce, aggregate, process, and package agroproducts for sell.
  3. We build creative platforms to engage youths in agro-related adventures.

Core Values: Creative, smart, dynamic, pragmatic, innovative and flexible.

Concept note: "Founded in 2019, Indus-Mega Farms is a gap-bridger between industrial
markets and farms in Nigeria. We encourage both small and large holder
farmers to produce better by creating an enabling working evironment with
zero-waste/funding farming system on ground. We also have built creative
platforms to engage youths in agro-related adventures."
